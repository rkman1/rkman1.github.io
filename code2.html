Verilog (In Progress)

Project that will utilize the VGA to display a frequency adjustable waveform whose frequency tone is outputted via an audio signal.

  • Components of the project:
    • Verilog coded within Intel Quartus Prime
    • The DE2-115 50 MHz clock will be used to create square waveforms
    • Push buttons will be utilized to increase and decrease the frequency of the waveforms, changing dynamically on the screen
    • The audio frequency will also change as the frequency is adjusted by the push buttons
    • The LCD screen will display the current frequency of the waveform displayed on the VGA
