Brushless DC Electric Motor

  • The project involved designing and constructing a brushless dc electric motor from scratch
  • All components were designed on SOLIDWORKS and 3-D printed excluding the speed controller, wires, and axle
    • 3 motors were created: 50, 25 and 12 windings versions
  • A MATLAB program was written that provided predictions of how different numbers of winding would affect the angular velocity of the wheel. A GUI was also implemented.
